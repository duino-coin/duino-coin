Duino-Coin Cookie Policy

Last updated and effective: July 23, 2023


Welcome to Duino-Coin!

Welcome to Duino-Coin! This Cookie Policy explains how Duino-Coin ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies on our website (the "Site") to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized services. By continuing to use our Site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.


What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide a personalized browsing experience. Cookies can also track website usage and help website owners understand how users interact with their site.


How We Use Cookies

Duino-Coin uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the Site. They enable basic features such as user authentication, session management, and security. Without these cookies, certain services on the Site may not function correctly.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and settings, such as language preferences and display settings. They enhance your browsing experience and make it more convenient for you to use the Site.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use third-party cookies from Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how users interact with the Site. This data helps us analyze website traffic, user behavior, and improve our services. Google Analytics cookies do not collect personal information that can identify you.
  • Advertising Cookies: Our Site uses Google AdSense on some pages to display relevant advertisements. AdSense cookies help us deliver targeted ads based on your interests and online activities. These cookies are placed by third-party advertisers and allow them to track your interactions with their ads.

Your Cookie Choices

By default, most web browsers accept cookies. However, you can modify your browser settings to control or block cookies. Please note that blocking essential cookies may impact the functionality and performance of the Site.


Third-Party Cookies

Please be aware that third-party services, such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense, may also use cookies to collect information about your browsing activities on the Site and other websites. These cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of these third-party providers.


Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we update the policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
